First records of the parasitoid wasp genus Saotis Förster, 1869 for Italy (Hymenoptera, Ichneumonidae, Ctenopelmatinae, Mesoleiini)

Authors: Dal Pos, D.

Published in: Bollettino del Museo di Storia Naturale di Venezia, 68: 59–62, 2017.

Download PDF

Abstract

The genus Saotis Förster, 1869, with its species Saotis mirabilis Schmiedeknecht, 1914, is hereby recorded for the first time from Italy..
