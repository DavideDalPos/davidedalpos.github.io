About Me

Hello! My name is Davide Dal Pos, and I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Central Florida, where I am exploring the evolutionary morphology of parasitoid wasps. My research interest spans across entomology and taxonomy (specifically of Ichneumoninae), databasing and collection management.
